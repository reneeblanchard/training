Come and Visit Renee's Free-Range Chickens!

Welcome to Renee's Free-Range Chicken Farm

We specialise in producing Free Range chickens. Reared and processed on our farm, over looking the Wye Valley. They are fed a cereal diet and allowed to roam freely and grown to full maturity. Feel free to come and visit us, feed our chickens and even hold them if you're brave enough.

We have lots of breeds of chicken...

  • Plymouth Rock
  • Rhode Island Red
  • Broiler
