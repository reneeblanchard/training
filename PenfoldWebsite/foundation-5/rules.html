  • Rules Of The Course

  • These rules must be followed at all times whilst playing at Penfold Park Golf Course. Failure to do so may end in the offender being asked to leave and not return.

  • No more than 4 players per game.
  • One set of clubs per person - NO BAG SHARING
  • Only ONE ball per player
  • Balls struck onto an adjoining green must be lifted and dropped, without penalty.
  • Shirts must be worn on the course at all times.
  • All players play on the course at their own risk.
  • Out of bounds - the confine of the course.
  • One round is 9 holes.
  • Tee mats must be used at all times - do not play tee shots from grass.
  • Do not retrieve golf balls from the horse field on 9th hole.
