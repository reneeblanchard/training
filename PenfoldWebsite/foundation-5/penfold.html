Penfold Park Golf Course

9 hole, par 30, beginners course.

Penfold Park is suitable for all ages, from young to old. We have a practice putting green for younger players and the course is flat making it perfect for older players. Everyone is welcome, just drop in and play; no need to book. We hire out clubs and trolleys for those who don't have their own. We also have a cafe area where you can grab yourself a hot sandwich or cold drink.
